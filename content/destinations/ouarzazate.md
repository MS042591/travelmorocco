Ouarzazate is the gateway to the Sahara and the Draa Valley. It is a city that feels like a film set, and for good reason—it has hosted some of the biggest productions in cinema history, from 'Gladiator' to 'Game of Thrones'.

The architecture here is dominated by the warm hues of adobe and pisé. The Kasbah Taourirt is one of the most impressive in the country. Ouarzazate is the perfect base for exploring the deep south and the surrounding oases.
