Marrakech is an assault on the senses in the most beautiful way. Known as the Red City for its rose-hued buildings, it serves as the cultural capital of Morocco. 

### Why Visit Marrakech?
Whether you are interested in history, architecture, or simply soaking up the atmosphere of a medieval city, Marrakech offers something for everyone. The city is a blend of ancient traditions and modern Moroccan luxury.

### Best Time to Visit
The best months to visit Marrakech are from March to May or between September and November when the weather is warm but not scorching.
