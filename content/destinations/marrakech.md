Marrakech, often known as the 'Red City' due to its terracotta-colored walls, is the heart of Moroccan tourism. It is a place where sensory overload is part of the charm. From the chaotic energy of the Jemaa el-Fnaa square at night to the tranquil beauty of the Majorelle Garden, Marrakech offers a contrast at every corner.

Explore the ancient Saadian Tombs, wander through the labyrinthine souks filled with the scent of spices and leather, and find peace in a luxury riad hidden behind an unassuming door. Marrakech is not just a city; it's an experience that stays with you long after you've left.
