Casablanca is the economic and industrial heart of Morocco, a city that perfectly blends Moroccan tradition with a fast-paced, modern lifestyle. While often seen as a business hub, it holds treasures like the Hassan II Mosque, one of the largest and most beautiful in the world, partially built over the sea.

Wander through the Habous quarter to see 'New Medina' architecture, or enjoy the sea breeze along the Corniche. Casablanca is the gateway to the kingdom and a testament to its future.
