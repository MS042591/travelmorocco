The Sahara is the highlight of many Moroccan journeys. The silence of the desert and the scale of the dunes are truly life-changing.

### Merzouga vs Zagora
Merzouga offers the iconic high orange dunes (Erg Chebbi), while Zagora is more rocky and accessible for shorter trips. We specialize in the Merzouga experience.

### What to Pack
The desert gets very cold at night even in summer, so bring layers. A scarf is also essential for protection against wind and sand.
