Tangier has always been a city of intrigue and mystery. Standing at the northern tip of Africa, it offers views across the water to Europe. It has served as an international zone and a muse for writers like Paul Bowles and the Beat Generation.

Today, Tangier is undergoing a grand revival, with a brand-new marina and restored historic sites. The medina is a fascinating place to get lost, leading up to the Kasbah with its panoramic views of the Mediterranean.
