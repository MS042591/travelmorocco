Merzouga is the stuff of legends. Located at the edge of the Erg Chebbi dunes, this small village is the starting point for most Saharan adventures. The dunes here can reach heights of 150 meters, changing color from pale gold to deep orange as the sun moves across the sky.

A night in the desert is an experience of profound silence and spectacular beauty. Sleep in a luxury tent, listen to Gnaoua music around a campfire, and witness a night sky so clear that the Milky Way feels close enough to touch.
