# Marrakech to Merzouga Desert Tour

Experience the magic of the Sahara desert with our 3-day tour from Marrakech to Merzouga.

## Your Journey

Stay in luxury Riads and experience the authentic Moroccan hospitality. Our expert guides will take you through the most scenic routes and hidden gems.

### Day 1: Marrakech - Ait Ben Haddou - Dades Valley
Departure from Marrakech over the High Atlas mountains via the Tizi n'Tichka pass. Visit the UNESCO World Heritage site of Ait Ben Haddou.

### Day 2: Dades Valley - Todra Gorge - Merzouga
After breakfast, we head to the Todra Gorge, a massive fault in the plateau separating the High Atlas from the Jbel Saghro.

### Day 3: Merzouga - Ouarzazate - Marrakech
Wake up early to catch the sunrise over the dunes of Merzouga. After breakfast and a shower, we start our journey back to Marrakech.
