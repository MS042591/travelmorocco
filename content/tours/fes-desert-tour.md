# Fes to Sahara Desert

A direct journey from the cultural heart of Fes to the golden dunes of Merzouga.

## Your Journey

### Day 1: Fes - Middle Atlas - Merzouga
Depart from Fes and head south, passing through Ifrane and the cedar forest of Azrou where you can see Barbary macaques.

### Day 2: Merzouga Area - Camel Trek
Explore the desert region, visit a nomadic family, and enjoy a Gnaoua music performance before embarking on a camel trek.

### Day 3: Merzouga - Rissani - Fes
After watching the sunrise, travel back to Fes via the Ziz Valley and the High Atlas mountains.

## Highlights
- Middle Atlas Mountains
- Cedar Forest (Barbary Macaques)
- Ziz Valley
- Erg Chebbi Dunes
