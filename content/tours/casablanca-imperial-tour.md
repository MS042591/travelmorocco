# Casablanca to Imperial Cities

Discover the rich history of Morocco's imperial cities starting from Casablanca.

## Your Journey

### Day 1: Arrival in Casablanca
Welcome to Morocco! Visit the majestic Hassan II Mosque, one of the largest in the world.

### Day 2: Casablanca - Rabat - Meknes
Head to Rabat to visit the Hassan Tower and Oudaya Kasbah, then continue to the imperial city of Meknes.

### Day 3: Meknes - Volubilis - Fes
Explore the Roman ruins of Volubilis and the holy city of Moulay Idriss before arriving in Fes.

### Day 4: Fes Medina Tour
A full day dedicated to exploring the oldest medina in the world with a local guide.

### Day 5: Fes - Beni Mellal - Marrakech
Travel through the Middle Atlas mountains and agricultural plains to reach Marrakech.

### Day 6: Marrakech City Tour
Visit the Bahia Palace, Saadian Tombs, and the vibrant Jemaa el-Fnaa square.

### Day 7: Departure from Marrakech
Final shopping in the souks and transfer to the airport for your flight home.
