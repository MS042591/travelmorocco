# Tangier to Chefchaouen & Fes

Explore the Blue City of Chefchaouen and the ancient city of Fes starting from Tangier.

## Your Journey

### Day 1: Arrival in Tangier
Welcome to the gateway of Africa. Explore the Kasbah and the Hercules Caves.

### Day 2: Tangier - Chefchaouen
Travel to the famous 'Blue Pearl' of Morocco and spend the afternoon wandering its cobalt streets.

### Day 3: Chefchaouen - Volubilis - Fes
Visit the Roman ruins of Volubilis before continuing to the cultural heart of Fes.

### Day 4: Fes Sightseeing & Departure
Discover the hidden gems of the Fes Medina before your departure or transfer.
